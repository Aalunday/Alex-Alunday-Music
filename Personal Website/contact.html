Lessons

Alex is available for online or in person lessons in the ABQ area. With these lessons they can range from classical or jazz saxophone, jazz piano, jazz theory for any instrument, and beginner flute and clarinet. You can also find him at Rock 101 teaching piano, saxophone, and programs such as the summer sessions and band league. In lessons you can expect a great deal of emphasis on playing creatively and striving to help you find your own voice.
